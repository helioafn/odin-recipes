Vatapá

Description

Vatapá is a delicious brazilian dish from Bahia, it's easy to make and combines with lots of other dishes

Ingredients

  • 5 french bread
  • 2 small onions
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup of azeite
  • Salt to taste

How to make Vatapá

  1. Soak the breads in water for 5 minutes.
  2. In the blender, beat 50 g of shrimp with a little water and sift the mixture.
  3. In the liquefier, beat the shrimp broth, onions, breads in pieces, peanuts, coconut milk and chestnut.
  4. Put salt to taste.
  5. Place the mixture in a saucepan over medium heat, add the palm oil and the rest of the shrimps.
  6. Stir for 20 minutes or until a firm mixture.
